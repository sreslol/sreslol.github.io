Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer telehealth visits?

Yes. All sessions are provided virtually through secure video, phone, or messaging based on what works best for you.

What languages do you speak?

How many years of experience do you have?

I have 20 years of clinical experience in mental health and social work.
